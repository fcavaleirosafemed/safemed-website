## 6. ALL MODULES - FULL CONTENT

### 6.1 Saude no Trabalho (Occupational Health)

**Page Tagline:** "Um software adaptado para responder a todas as necessidades de Saude do Trabalhado e dos seus trabalhadores."

**Module Description:** "A saude no trabalho tem como objetivo salvaguardar o bem-estar global dos trabalhadores, abordando fatores fisicos, mentais e sociais no ambiente laboral."

**Features (9 detailed):**

1. **Ficha Clinica Parametrizavel** (Customizable Clinical File)
   - Centralizes all worker clinical processes dynamically
   - Allows parameterization based on clinical staff needs

2. **Ficha de Aptidao Digital** (Digital Fitness Certificate)
   - Eliminates paper-based fitness certificates
   - Fully digital completion with multi-party digital signatures
   - Workers can independently access and extract certificates

3. **Agendamentos** (Scheduling)
   - Monitors expiring fitness certificates
   - Automated email and SMS convocations
   - Alerts for required scheduling

4. **Gestao de Calendarios de Medicos e Enfermeiros** (Healthcare Professional Scheduling)
   - Manages medical and nursing staff availability calendars
   - Enables efficient appointment scheduling

5. **Riscos por posto de trabalho** (Workplace Hazard Assessment)
   - Displays worker exposure risks immediately
   - Automatically transfers risk data to fitness certificates
   - Visibility for doctors, safety technicians, and workers

6. **Historico de Consultas** (Consultation History)
   - Tracks all consultations and examinations over time
   - Records performer and results
   - Provides quick activity overview

7. **Assinatura digital certificada** (Certified Digital Signature)
   - Supports citizen card, Medical Association card, and digitizer signatures
   - Eliminates paper documentation

8. **Notificacao por SMS** (SMS Notifications)
   - One-click mass SMS to scheduled workers
   - Appointment reminder functionality

9. **Declaracoes de Presenca** (Attendance Declarations)
   - Generates editable documents
   - Records worker arrival and departure times

**Additional feature mentioned on homepage:** Relatorio Unico - Anexo D

### 6.2 Seguranca no Trabalho (Workplace Safety)

**Module Description:** "A seguranca do trabalho dedica-se a garantir a protecao dos trabalhadores no ambiente laboral." Focuses on identifying, evaluating, and controlling workplace risks through norms, procedures, and training.

**Features (9 detailed):**

1. **Gestao de Auditorias** (Audit Management)
   "Controlo de auditorias e do plano anual de atividades a executar de acordo com as caracteristicas cada empresa."
   - Risk assessment audits, compliance checks, machine evaluations
   - Intelligent scheduling mechanisms

2. **Agendamentos** (Smart Scheduling)
   "Mecanismo inteligente da gestao de agendamentos por drag&drop, que permite agendar facilmente qualquer tipo de consulta/exame."
   - Drag-and-drop calendar by professional or room

3. **Mapa com localizacao geografica de Visitas** (Geographic Visit Mapping)
   - Displays all daily appointments
   - Calculates optimal visit routes to save time and resources

4. **Relatorios Parametrizaveis** (Customizable Reports)
   "Sistematize os relatorios de risco, conformidade e outros relatorios consoante os objetivos e necessidades da sua empresa."
   - Intelligent learning mechanism evolves reports autonomously

5. **Notificacao de Atividades** (Activity Notifications)
   - Automatic alerts for scheduled audits and visits

6. **Gestao Documental** (Document Management)
   "Com o Safemed armazene e organize toda a documentacao de Seguranca da empresa de forma intuitiva e preservando o ambiente."
   - Centralized storage, on-demand access

7. **Gestao de EPI** (PPE Management)
   - Assignment tracking, workplace/task relationships
   - Reception documentation with signature capability

8. **Gestao de Acidentes de Trabalho** (Accident Management)
   - Accident records, approval workflows, incident characterization
   - Official accident reports, multi-user submission

9. **Medidas de Autoprotecao** (Self-Protection Measures)
   - Auto-protection plans with dynamic registration
   - Monitoring, inspections, training records, emergency drills

## 7. INDUSTRIES / TARGET SECTORS - FULL CONTENT

### 7.1 Prestacao de Servicos (Service Providers)
"Em Portugal, as empresas prestadoras de servicos de saude e seguranca no trabalho desempenham um papel crucial na conformidade legal e na protecao dos trabalhadores."
- Target: Companies providing OHS services in Portugal (Lei 102/2009)
- Value props: Client & contract lifecycle management, administrative automation, operational control (profitability, fees, consultation times), direct client communication (Portal de Cliente, My Safemed, Easy Booking), statistical analysis via Kube

### 7.2 Centros Hospitalares e Unidades Locais de Saude (Healthcare Centers)
"Os trabalhadores enfrentam uma serie de desafios ocupacionais unicos devido a natureza do ambiente de saude."
- Target: Portuguese hospital centers and local health units
- Value props: Privacy & data segregation, integration with SNS/Sclinic/hospital systems, cross-team information sharing, protection from pathogen exposure

### 7.3 Industria (Manufacturing)
"Os trabalhadores estao expostos a uma variedade de riscos ocupacionais especificos, desde a manipulacao de equipamentos pesados ate o manuseio de produtos quimicos e substancias nocivas."
- Target: Automotive, food processing, manufacturing
- Value props: Multi-discipline health records, equipment protection + chemical management, HR integration, paperless safety

### 7.4 Construcao (Construction)
"Esta industria e conhecida por ambientes de trabalho dinamicos e desafiadores, onde os trabalhadores estao expostos a uma ampla gama de riscos ocupacionais."
- Target: Construction companies
- Value props: Project management (obras, worker circulation), fitness record enforcement, PPE management per worker/function, falls/heavy equipment/hazardous substance management

### 7.5 Associacoes Empresariais (Business Associations)
- Target: Portuguese business associations
- Value props: Member + quota management, centralized contact management, same operational features as Service Providers, Kube analytics

### 7.6 Aviacao Civil (Civil Aviation)
- Target: Civil aviation sector
- Value props: Aviation medicine management (pilot health surveillance), safety team workflows, Kube analytics (risk-health correlation), "Fast Delivery" - full deployment in 6 weeks
